Our General Medicine Services

GENERAL HEALTH CHECK-UP

A comprehensive evaluation of your overall health, including physical examinations, blood tests, and vital sign assessments. This check-up helps in early detection of potential health issues and promotes preventive care for a healthier lifestyle.

CARDIOVASCULAR DISEASES

Cardiovascular diseases encompass a range of conditions affecting the heart and blood vessels, including heart disease, hypertension, and stroke. Regular screenings and consultations can help identify risk factors early, enabling effective management and prevention strategies for better heart health.

Liver Diseases / Kidney Diseases

Liver and kidney diseases can significantly impact overall health, affecting the body’s ability to filter toxins and manage essential functions. Conditions such as hepatitis, cirrhosis, and chronic kidney disease may be asymptomatic in early stages.

DIABETES/ HYPERTENSION/ DYSLIPIDEMIA

A diabetes checkup involves monitoring blood sugar levels and assessing overall health to manage diabetes effectively. It helps identify potential complications early and tailor treatment plans for better health outcomes.

EYE, EAR, NOSE & THROAT INFECTIONS

Eye, ear, nose, and throat infections can cause symptoms like pain, swelling, and inflammation in these areas. Prompt diagnosis and treatment are essential to prevent complications and promote recovery.

SKIN DISEASE

Skin diseases encompass a wide range of conditions, including infections, rashes, and chronic disorders like eczema and psoriasis. Proper diagnosis and treatment are crucial for managing symptoms and improving skin health.

THYROID DISEASE

Thyroid disease affects hormone production, leading to conditions like hypothyroidism or hyperthyroidism. Monitoring and treatment are essential for maintaining metabolic balance and overall well-being.

ENDOCRINOLOGY

Endocrinology focuses on hormone-related disorders, including diabetes, thyroid issues, and adrenal conditions. Specialists work to diagnose and manage hormonal imbalances for optimal health.

ARTHRITIS

Arthritis includes conditions like osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis, leading to joint pain and inflammation. Comprehensive pain management strategies are essential for enhancing mobility and quality of life.

PILES,FISSURES,HEMORRHOIDS

Piles, fissures, and hemorrhoids cause discomfort and bleeding in the anal area, often requiring lifestyle changes and medical treatment. Effective management helps alleviate symptoms and improve quality of life.

MINOR INJURY AND FIRST, SECOND DEGREE BURNS MANAGEMENT

Management of minor injuries and burns involves assessing and treating wounds to prevent infection and promote healing. Proper care ensures comfort and minimizes complications.

ALLERGY,ASTHMA AND OTHER RESPIRATORY DISEASES

Allergy, asthma, and respiratory diseases involve immune responses and airway inflammation, causing symptoms like wheezing and difficulty breathing. Comprehensive care and management are vital for improving quality of life.

SEXUAL HEALTH

Sexual health encompasses a range of issues, including sexual function, sexually transmitted infections, and reproductive health. Education and access to care are key for maintaining healthy sexual well-being.

LABORATORY SERVICES

Laboratory services provide essential diagnostic testing, including blood work, urine tests, and imaging. These tests aid in the accurate diagnosis and monitoring of various health conditions.
